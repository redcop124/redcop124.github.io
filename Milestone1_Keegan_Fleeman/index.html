About Us

This is Kenensaw State University's CARIT homepage. CARIT stands for Center for Applied Research in Information Technology. We launched this site to better interact with our local businesses and communities. The University of North Carolina Wilmington has a great idea to launch a CARIT site, and it was a wild success. Based upon their model, we decided to launch our own to better connect with our communities. Here is the link to UNC's model site: https://uncw.edu/ssarc/

Thank you for visiting KSU's CARIT! Visit us at http://ccse.kennesaw.edu/it
